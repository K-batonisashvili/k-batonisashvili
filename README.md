My name is Kote Batonisashvili and I am a systems, robotics, and automation engineer who's passionate about creating reliable infrastructure, automating redundancies, breaking things, then fixing them. I recently graduated *Magna cum laude* from Boston University with an M.S. in Mechanical Engineering and a Robotics Specialization. 

Before transitioning into robotics, I spent over 7 years architecting enterprise-scale IT environments, managing zero-trust infrastructure, and building event-driven automation pipelines for organizations supporting 20,000+ endpoints. Today, I combine my rigorous academic background in algorithmic path-planning, computer vision, and machine learning with my decade of industry experience to write safe, scalable, and resilient code.
